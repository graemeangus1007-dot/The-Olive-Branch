Accessibility

We want every guest to feel welcome—online and in the café.

Our Commitment

The Olive Branch Cafe strives to make this website usable for as many people as possible, including guests who use assistive technologies.

Website Experience

Pages include skip links, keyboard-friendly navigation, descriptive labels, and support for reduced-motion preferences. We continue improving contrast, focus states, and content clarity over time.

Visiting the Café

If you need assistance planning a visit, ordering, or navigating our space, please reach out and we will do our best to help.
